§1 — Recitals

About Rochelle

WHEREAS contract lifecycle management, legal program management, and global compliance are too often treated as three separate disciplines — I have spent the last sixteen years proving they are one practice, navigated together.

I've built that practice inside Meta and Amazon, inside global law firms like Wilson Sonsini, K&L Gates, and Hogan Lovells, and inside federal government programs — which means I've seen the same intake, risk, and governance problems from the in-house side, the firm side, and the regulated side. I know which solution travels across all three, and which one only works in just one of them.

My work is the operational backbone underneath legal teams: the templates, triage logic, approval routing, and dashboards that turn "legal review" from a bottleneck into a system anyone can trust.
